PolyGen has been deployed across EMF Camp! 

Grab your RFID tokens from PolyGen HQ located nextdoor to Spark Life.  You can take a white token for free, or buy one of the custom RFID tokens.  These will be relocated to the bar in Null Sector once it opens.

The game is operational and can be played between 21:00 on Thursday and 22:00 on Sunday.  Pick your team and capture as many units as possible.

The only scoreboard and player portal will be operational during Friday (the backend server had to be rebuilt from scratch).
