Polybius Biotech calls on everyone at EMF Camp to take control of the deployed PolyGen portable power generators and direct the power to the team of their choice.  You can use most normal RFID cards that you already have, or you'll be able to pick up a free RFID card from the bar in Nullsector or PolyGen HQ (location to be confirmed).  There will be themed 3D printed tokens available to buy if you want something a bit more special.

For anyone wanting to see inside the game there is a talk you can attend.  [PolyGen Talk](https://www.emfcamp.org/schedule/2026/293-polygen-inside-the-game).  Those attending will get a unique PolyGen unit to interact with only available during the talk.

## Malevolent Enterprises
![Malevolent Enterprises](https://gen.polyb.io/assets/img/Malevolent.png "Malevolent Enterprises")

Associated Colour: Teal

If there is a dubious industry that many brands would shy away from, you can expect Malevolent Enterprises to have a stake in it.

## ChimeraTech Corporation
![ChimeraTech Corporation](https://gen.polyb.io/assets/img/ChimeraTech.png "ChimeraTech Corporation")

Associated Colour: Yellow

Known for their biolabs and the many wonderful creations that have emerged.  Seldom does anyone list having worked at ChimeraTech on the employment history, it is unclear if that is because they stay for life or just never actually leave.

## Blackspire Consortium
![Blackspire Consortium](https://gen.polyb.io/assets/img/BlackSpire.png "Blackspire Consortium")

Associated Colour: Pink

The consortium formed bring together many smaller organisations that had been targeted by Malevolant Enterprises, with both organisations having a significant overlap in the areas they operate in. Don't expect any love to be lost between Black Spire and Malevolant Enterprises. 

## Nothing To See Here Industries
![Nothing To See Here Industries](https://gen.polyb.io/assets/img/NothingToSeeHere.png "Nothing To See Here Industries")

Associated Colour: Green

Little is known about this organisation.  They are highly profitable and no one is really sure why or how.
